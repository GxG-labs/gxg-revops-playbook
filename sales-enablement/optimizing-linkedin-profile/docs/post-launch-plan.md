# After Profile Update Plan

Use this after the profile rewrite is complete. A polished profile without activity is a static asset; this turns it into a channel.

## First 48 Hours

1. Review profile on desktop and mobile.
2. Check first impression: photo, banner, headline, current role, location.
3. Click every Featured link.
4. Confirm contact info and public profile visibility.
5. Turn Open to Work or Services on only after profile quality is acceptable.
6. Ask 3 trusted people for profile feedback.
7. Send 3-5 recommendation requests.

## First 7 Days

Career mode:
- Connect with 20 relevant recruiters/sourcers/hiring managers.
- Reconnect with 15 former colleagues.
- Comment on 10 posts from target companies or market leaders.
- Apply to target roles through company sites, not only Easy Apply.
- Send referral requests where warm paths exist.

B2B mode:
- Identify 50 target accounts or buyer profiles.
- Follow 20 high-signal ICP voices.
- Comment meaningfully 5-10 times per day.
- Publish 1 proof-oriented post.
- Send 5 warm reactivation messages.

## 30-Day Cadence

Weekly:
- 2-3 posts or 1 strong post plus daily comments
- 30-50 meaningful comments
- 20-40 new targeted connections
- 5-10 warm DMs
- 1 new proof asset or Featured improvement
- Analytics review

## Content Plan

### Pillar Mix

Career mode:
- Role expertise
- Project proof
- Lessons learned
- Market observations
- Portfolio walkthroughs

B2B mode:
- ICP pain
- Diagnostic frameworks
- Case studies
- Point of view
- Tactical fixes
- Offer-adjacent education

## Networking Motions

### Recruiter Motion

1. Identify recruiters hiring for target role.
2. Follow and lightly engage before DM when possible.
3. Send concise message:

```text
Hi {Name}, I saw you recruit for {role/domain}. I work in {target role/specialty}, with recent focus on {proof}. If you are covering roles around {target}, happy to be on your radar.
```

### Referral Motion

```text
I'm exploring {target role} roles at {company/market}. Given your context there, would you be open to pointing me to the right person or sharing what the team values in candidates?
```

### Buyer Motion

```text
Your post about {topic} caught my attention. I see the same issue in {ICP}: {specific observation}. I put together a short checklist for diagnosing {problem}; happy to send it if useful.
```

## Analytics Loop

Review weekly:

| Metric | If Low | Fix |
|--------|--------|-----|
| Search appearances | Keywords/search settings weak | Update headline, skills, target titles, location |
| Profile views | Result card weak | Improve photo, headline, current role |
| Messages/replies | Profile proof weak | Improve About, Featured, Experience, CTA |
| Connection acceptance | Audience/message mismatch | Tighten targeting and note |
| Recruiter relevance | Keywords too broad/wrong | Remove misleading skills/titles |
| Buyer relevance | Positioning too vague | Sharpen ICP, pain, offer |

## Maintenance

Monthly:
- Refresh Featured
- Add new proof
- Reorder skills
- Update current role bullets
- Review search appearances
- Remove stale positioning
- Ask for one new recommendation after meaningful work
