# Career Mode Sub-Skill

Use this when optimizing for recruiters, sourcers, hiring managers, referrals, target vacancies, or job-search inbound.

## Recruiter Search Model

Recruiters search and filter by:
- Job title
- Current/past company
- Location
- Skills
- Industry
- Years/seniority proxies
- Education
- Spoken languages
- Open to Work
- Keywords and Boolean strings
- AI-assisted natural-language search

The profile must support both exact filters and human judgment.

## Target Vacancy Workflow

1. Collect 10-20 target job descriptions.
2. Extract:
   - Titles
   - Must-have skills
   - Nice-to-have skills
   - Tools
   - Industry terms
   - Seniority signals
   - Business outcomes
3. Build keyword map.
4. Rewrite headline and About around the most repeated terms.
5. Rewrite current and last two roles first.
6. Reorder skills.
7. Align resume and LinkedIn chronology.
8. Set Open to Work and job preferences.
9. Create referral/networking plan.

## Open To Work Guidance

Use **Recruiters only** when:
- Currently employed
- Senior/executive search
- Concerned about public optics
- Scam/noise risk is high

Use **public badge** when:
- Actively unemployed or publicly searching
- Network can help
- The profile is polished
- Public signaling is culturally acceptable in the target market

Set:
- 3-5 target job titles
- Accurate locations
- Remote/hybrid/on-site preferences
- Employment types
- Start date

## Resume and LinkedIn Alignment

Flag:
- Different titles at same company
- Different dates beyond month-level tolerance
- Missing roles
- LinkedIn roles absent from resume without reason
- Resume keywords absent from LinkedIn
- LinkedIn headline targeting a role not supported by resume proof

## Recruiter Conversion Checklist

- Headline says the target role
- Current role supports the target role
- About first two lines are relevant to the target role
- Skills match the job description
- Experience has proof, not only responsibilities
- Featured contains portfolio/case proof
- Recommendations support trust
- Contact path is clear
- No contradictions with resume
