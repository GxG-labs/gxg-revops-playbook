# B2B Sales Mode Sub-Skill

Use this when optimizing LinkedIn for clients, consulting, founder-led sales, executive credibility, partnerships, referrals, or social selling.

## Buyer Search and Trust Model

B2B buyers rarely search like recruiters. They notice:
- A comment under someone they trust
- A post shared by a peer
- A referral or intro
- A clear category in the headline
- Proof in Featured
- A specific About section
- Repeated useful ideas over time

The profile must answer:
- What do you do?
- Who do you help?
- What problem do you solve?
- Why should I trust you?
- What should I do next?

## Featured Plan

Use Featured as the buyer proof shelf:

1. Case study with quantified or concrete result
2. Diagnostic checklist
3. Offer or booking page
4. Thought-leadership post with useful framework
5. Artifact sample: anonymized dashboard, workflow, playbook, teardown

## Content Pillars

Choose 3-5:
- Pain education: why the problem happens
- Diagnostic: how to spot the problem
- Case proof: before/after stories
- Point of view: what most people get wrong
- Tactical how-to: useful steps
- Tool/process teardown
- Market commentary
- Founder/operator lessons

## Comment Strategy

Daily/weekly comment targets:
- ICP leaders
- Category creators
- Recruiters/partners if hybrid
- Companies in target market
- Former colleagues and warm network

Comment rule:
- Add a specific observation, example, or counterpoint.
- Avoid generic agreement.
- Do not pitch in comments.

## DM Strategy

Use soft, context-aware messages.

Warm reactivation:

```text
Saw your post about {topic}. It reminded me of a pattern I keep seeing in {market}: {specific observation}. Curious if this is showing up for your team too.
```

Buyer intro:

```text
Noticed {trigger}. I work with {ICP} on {problem}. If useful, I can send a short checklist we use to diagnose {pain}.
```

Referral:

```text
I'm focusing on {ICP/problem}. If someone in your network is dealing with {trigger}, I would appreciate an intro. Happy to make it easy with a short forwardable note.
```

## B2B Conversion Metrics

Track:
- Profile views from target roles
- Connection acceptance
- Reply rate
- Calls booked
- Intro requests
- Post saves/comments from ICP
- Featured clicks
- Inbound messages mentioning a specific post or proof asset
