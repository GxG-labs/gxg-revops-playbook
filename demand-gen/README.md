# Demand Generation

Demand Generation skills cover content, inbound and outbound strategy, ICP definition, lead scoring, MQL/SQL criteria, ABM, and demand creation workflows.

This domain now includes a five-step Russian SEO/AEO/GEO content workflow: research, article architecture, drafting, optimization, and final quality audit.
