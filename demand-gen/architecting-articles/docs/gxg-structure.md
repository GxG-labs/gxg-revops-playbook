# GxG Article Structure Patterns

## Common H1 Formula

- `[Topic]: как [do practical action], [without common failure]`
- `[Topic] в 2026 году: как [reader goal]`
- `[Concept A] vs [Concept B]: как [choose/use/understand]`
- `[Role/tool/process]: ключевые навыки, задачи и ошибки`

## Opening

Good openings usually do one of these:

- name a familiar failure;
- show why old logic stopped working;
- contrast visible metrics with real business impact;
- explain why the reader notices the problem too late.

Use 2-4 short paragraphs before the first H2.

## Core Section Types

- Definition: "что это такое простыми словами".
- Difference: "чем отличается от..."
- Mechanics: "как это работает".
- Segmentation: by audience, funnel stage, lifecycle stage, channel, role, or use case.
- Metrics: what to measure and what not to overvalue.
- Mistakes: why common practice fails.
- Tools: named options and when they fit.
- Checklist: practical readiness check.
- FAQ: direct answers to concrete questions.

## Native Insert

GxG articles often place a vacancy block in the middle after several educational sections. Keep it natively relevant:

- marketing article -> `## Вакансии для маркетологов`;
- design article -> `## Вакансии для дизайнеров`;
- analytics article -> `## Вакансии для аналитиков`;
- development article -> `## Вакансии для разработчиков`.

If no job block is appropriate, use a short product/helpful resource insertion instead.

## Tone Of Structure

- Practical, not academic.
- Explains tradeoffs.
- Uses "почему", "как", "когда", "что ломает", "что делать".
- Avoids empty motivational conclusions.
