# SEO/AEO/GEO Editor Checklist

## SEO

- H1 contains the topic and user outcome.
- First paragraphs clarify why the topic matters now.
- H2 sections cover the main sub-intents.
- Related entities and synonyms are present naturally.
- Suggested slug is readable and specific.
- Meta title is under roughly 60 characters when possible.
- Meta description is under roughly 155-165 characters when possible.
- Internal link ideas are relevant.

## AEO

- Definitions are direct.
- FAQ answers can stand alone.
- Lists and tables answer concrete questions.
- The article has "what is", "how it works", "mistakes", "tools", and "checklist" coverage when relevant.

## GEO

- Paragraphs are self-contained enough to be cited.
- Statistics and claims have source notes.
- Comparisons explicitly name the compared concepts.
- The article avoids vague universal claims.
- The article includes concrete steps or criteria.

## Structured Data Suggestions

Suggest only when the visible content supports it:

- `Article`
- `FAQPage`
- `BreadcrumbList`
- `HowTo` only for genuine step-by-step instructions.

Prefer JSON-LD in implementation notes.
